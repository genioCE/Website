Industry Context

Global supply chains move at the speed of expectation—yet data silos slow every handoff. TMS feeds, telematics streams, WMS events, and ERP orders speak different languages. The result: blind spots, OTIF penalties, and rising carbon scrutiny.

Genio Logistics unifies these signals into a recursive memory engine, giving operators, planners, and executives one continuously updating narrative of movement and risk.

The Genio Logistics Loop

NOW → EXPRESS

  • Consumes EDI/API feeds from TMS, GPS/IoT telematics, WMS scans, yard sensors, and customer order portals in real‑time.
  • Auto‑harmonizes SKUs, locations, and time zones across partners.

INTERPRET → REFLECT

  • Detects route deviations, dwell inflation, and temperature excursions using anomaly models.
  • Correlates weather, port congestion, and driver HOS to forecast service risk.

VISUALIZE → EMBED

  • Streams dynamic ETA updates, cost‑to‑serve heatmaps, and carbon dashboards to control towers.
  • Embeds every milestone as a vector for semantic search across lanes and SKUs.

REPLAY

  • Instant playback of any shipment journey—proof for customers, auditors, and continuous improvement teams.
  • Feeds root‑cause learnings into future tendering, load planning, and network design.

Focused Modules

1. Fleet Intelligence

Integrates engine diagnostics, driver behavior, and fuel telemetry. Predictive alerts reduce breakdowns and optimize maintenance windows—cutting downtime and emissions.

2. Warehouse Vision

Computer‑vision feeds from dock doors track pallet flow and dwell. Genio flags bottlenecks, guiding task interleaving to lift throughput without extra headcount.

3. Cold Chain Assurance

Captures real‑time temperature/humidity probes. Autonomously suggests reroutes or cross‑docks to protect product integrity and service levels.

4. Sustainability Ledger

Aggregates fuel burn, idle time, and mode splits. Calculates shipment‑level CO2e, auto‑populating ESG disclosures and carbon marketplace offsets.

Architecture Snapshot

  • Edge Ingest: REST/EDI gateways + MQTT brokers feed Apache Kafka topics.
  • Stream OPS: Flink joins events with geo‑fences and SLA rules in milliseconds.
  • Genio Heads: NER (SKU & lane extraction), Sentiment (driver notes), QA, Embed—all containerized with NeMo 2.0 and deployable on GPUs, TPUs, LPUs.
  • Memory Layer: Postgres (events), Qdrant (vectors), DuckDB (time‑series) for instant replay and analytics.
  • Visualization: Mapbox GL lane viewer, Plotly KPI boards, with XR plugs for digital twins of distribution centers.
