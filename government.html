Public-Sector Landscape

Worldwide, governments invest $8 T+ annually in IT and infrastructure. Yet traffic flows, utility grids, health systems, and permitting still run on siloed data. Policy makers face lagging dashboards and fragmented insights—while citizens demand real-time transparency and resilience.

HNAI Gov interlinks these civic streams into a continuous memory engine, turning raw signals into actionable policy, budget, and service decisions.

The HNAI Civic Loop

NOW → EXPRESS

  • Ingests IoT sensors (traffic, air quality, energy meters), 311 calls, hospital feeds, and open-data portals in real time.
  • Geocodes and time-syncs events across agencies.

INTERPRET → REFLECT

  • Detects congestion, outage clusters, disease signals, and budget drift with multivariate models.
  • Runs scenario simulations—e.g., lane closures, vaccination drives—to predict impact before deployment.

VISUALIZE → EMBED

  • Streams live heat-maps, KPI scorecards, and what-if sliders to city command centers.
  • Embeds every event and simulation as vectors for cross-department recall.

REPLAY

  • Timeline playback of crises, interventions, and outcomes—fueling after-action reviews and grant reporting.
  • Memory feeds inform capital planning, zoning updates, and citizen-engagement tools.

Strategic Modules

1. Urban Mobility Optimizer

Merges traffic sensors, transit schedules, and ride-share APIs. Suggests signal timing, bus-lane adjustments, and micro-mobility incentives—cutting commute times and emissions.

2. Public Health Sentinel

Integrates hospital ER feeds, wastewater surveillance, and pharmacy sales. Early-warns outbreaks, allocates PPE, and evaluates intervention efficacy.

3. Smart Utilities Balance

Combines electric, water, and gas telemetry with weather and market pricing. Recommends demand response, leak detection, and renewable dispatch.

4. Emergency Response Replay

Captures CAD logs, drone footage, and citizen alerts. Enables AR playback for training and improves resource staging for future events.

Architecture Snapshot

  • Edge Capture: LoRaWAN, 5 G, and OPC-UA gateways feed encrypted Kafka clusters.
  • HNAI Heads: Summarizer, NER (assets & locations), Sentiment (citizen feedback), Policy-QA, Embed—deployable on GPUs/TPUs/LPUs.
  • Memory Layer: PostGIS-enabled Postgres (spatial events), Qdrant (vectors), DuckDB (time-series).
  • Visualization: CesiumJS 3-D city twin, Plotly dashboards, and XR ops rooms.
