Industry Context

Modern BFSI operates at algorithmic speed, yet legacy cores, siloed ledgers, and manual reviews still shape daily decisions. Risk officers, underwriters, and fraud teams juggle KYC docs, transaction graphs, policy files, and regulatory updates—each trapped in its own system.

Genio BFSI unifies these fragments into a recursive memory engine. Every payment, claim, and compliance alert becomes part of a continuously learning truth graph—slashing risk, improving customer trust, and accelerating growth.

The Genio BFSI Loop

NOW → EXPRESS

  • Ingests core banking transactions, real-time payments, policy-admin feeds, market data, and document repositories.
  • Maps account, customer, and entity IDs across lines of business.

INTERPRET → REFLECT

  • Applies graph analytics for AML, sanctions, and relationship risk scoring.
  • Runs anomaly models on claims frequency, card usage, and trading patterns.

VISUALIZE → EMBED

  • Streams fraud alerts, risk heat-maps, and personalized insights to frontline systems.
  • Embeds each event as a vector—enabling semantic recall across decades of records.

REPLAY

  • Full audit trail of every decision—explainable AI for regulators and auditors.
  • Feeds resolved fraud cases and claims disputes back into training loops.

Architecture Snapshot

  • Secure Ingest: ISO 20022 / ISO 8583, FIX feeds, and SFTP docs stream into encrypted Kafka tiers.
  • Genio Heads: Summarizer, NER (entities & regs), Sentiment (customer comms), Risk-QA, Embed—containerized via NeMo 2.0 on GPUs, TPUs, or LPUs.
  • Memory Layer: PostgreSQL (ledger & policy), Qdrant (vectors), DuckDB (time-series) under strict role-based access.
  • Visualization: Neo4j Bloom risk maps, D3 credit dashboards, and VR-room mock stress-tests.
