Building the cognitive layer for enterprise intelligence

Genio is a cognitive operating system designed to help organizations think clearly at scale. It structures memory, integrates insight across systems, and supports learning over time.

  • Enterprise Intelligence: Genio captures and organizes knowledge, surfaces timely insights, and enables intelligent reuse across operations and strategy.
  • Strategic Alignment: Inspired by the principles of Requisite Organization and Knowledge Management, Genio delivers clarity across teams, timelines, and decisions.
  • Cloud & Compute Flexibility: Deployable across Azure, AWS, GCP, IBM Cloud, Oracle Cloud, and hybrid environments.
  • Hardware & Model Agnostic: Compatible with NVIDIA, AMD, Groq LPUs, TPUs, and emerging compute accelerators.
  • Modular Intelligence: From journaling and memory to SCADA and strategic modeling, Genio adapts to your most complex needs.
